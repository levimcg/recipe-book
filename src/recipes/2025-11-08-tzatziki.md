ingredients:
  - 250 grams plain Greek yogurt
  - 50 grams mayonaise
  - 1 clove of garlic
  - 1-2 persian cucumbers seeds removed
  - 1 teaspoon dried dill
  - 5 grams minced fresh parsley
  - 3 grams MSG
  - 15-20g Water or milk 
tags:
    - dips
    - sauces
    - greek
---
## Method
Combine all the ingredients in a small bowl and stir to combine. Use water or milk to thin the sauce to your desired consistency.

## Notes
Try to do this a head of time because it gets better after sitting for an hour or two.
